# Privacy Policy for Translate Pilot

**Last updated: March 07, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

Translate Pilot is designed to help you translate text, voice, and images quickly and efficiently.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

---

## 2. Data Collection

We **do not collect, store, or share any personal data** from users.

All translations (text, voice, images) are processed only to provide the core functionality of the app and are **not stored on our servers**.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

The app may use trusted third-party services to improve performance and reliability. These services may collect limited technical data.

Third-party providers used in the app:

* Google Play Services
* AdMob
* Firebase Analytics

You can review their privacy policies here:

* https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/
* https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543
* https://firebase.google.com/policies/analytics

---

## 4. Log Data

In case of errors, we may collect technical information through third-party tools.

This may include:

* Device type
* OS version
* App configuration
* Time and date of use
* IP address

This data is used only for debugging and improving app performance.

---

## 5. Cookies

Translate Pilot does not use cookies directly.

However, third-party services integrated into the app may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and service improvement.

---

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and ensure secure usage of the app.

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
